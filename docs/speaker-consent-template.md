## What this is

You are being asked to share your voice and your knowledge of **[LANGUAGE]** for the purpose of creating teaching tools and preserving the language for future generations.

## What will happen

- Your voice will be recorded.
- Recordings will be transcribed.
- Recordings and transcriptions will be used to train an AI model that can speak in your voice and help others learn the language.
- The model will be held by **[COMMUNITY / LANGUAGE AUTHORITY]**, not by any outside person or company.

## Your rights

- **You decide what topics to discuss.** You can refuse any prompt at any time.
- **You decide what is restricted.** Anything you mark as restricted will not be used in training. This includes sacred content, ceremonial language, family stories, or anything else you specify.
- **You can withdraw consent at any time.** Your recordings will be removed and the model will be retrained without your data.
- **You will be credited by name** (or by an alias you choose) for as long as your voice is in the system.
- **No commercial use** of recordings or models without separate, additional consent and a benefit-sharing agreement.
- **Your voice will not be combined with other voices** to create a new "actor" unless you specifically agree to that use.

## What we will not do

- Use your voice to say things you did not say, in any context outside the agreed teaching purpose, without additional consent.
- Share your recordings outside the community.
- Use restricted content for any purpose.
- Pass your voice or recordings to any government, university, or company without your written consent.
